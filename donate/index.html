To support Gospelgo, send funds to the solo developer. More options will be provided when appropriate. Any money given via the paypal link will be used for Gospelgo related expenses or saved for future expenses. Read below for more details.

Staff and Costs

I develop the games and this website in my free time after work. I plan to continue whether or not people give. I pay for all the software assets, domain purchases and other subscriptions/costs required to keep Gospelgo running out of pocket and honestly it's currently not all that expensive. The costs are similar to an expensive hobby. Costs will increase as Gospelgo grows.

Give for future needs

More players = more cost (that's good!). The costs for traffic to this website and each game, cloud save and other backend services have been low. As more players come in, network traffic thresholds will be exceeded which will trigger several monthly payments, probably all at once. At some point, I may not be able to cover all the costs. Pray for enough players for this to be the case! Also pray the games are actually good and glorifying God so the players are warranted. Not for the numbers but for God's kingdom and the gospel to change lives.
