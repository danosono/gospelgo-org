Why are Gospelgo games not on gaming platforms?

The biggest reason Gospelgo games are not primarily offered on popular gaming platforms is the enormous amount of work and energy required to publish on such platforms and each one has different requirements. There are other reasons such as avoidance of gatekeeping and freedom to develop and deploy in unique ways. The platforms are robust and impressive, but they are not a great fit for Gopselgo which is kind of sad because the platforms have large audiences. Gosple go does have 3 games on Steam (Word, Word2 and DOC ATI) Fortunately, web browsers are much more powerful now and that is on increasing with Web GPU becoming available on major browsers.
