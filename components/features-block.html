Word has many unique features

Word has many options that help you read God's word more often, have fun challenging yourself, keep a record of your progress and more, all while you improve your typing skills and speed.

Remembers WPM for each level

Each level (uually 2 verses) has it's own score and Word records this so you can review your previous scores. Try to go back and beat your old WPM!

Learn More

Regular Bible reading

Stop typing anytime and you can take time to read the current scripture. After 5 seconds of no typing, Word will roll back 5 seconds so your WPM is not affected. Take all the time you need! A message will be displayed onscreen indicating the game is in idle mode.

Learn More

Options!

Word has many options that were added based on player feedback. Want to pause between levels to see your score? Turn off Auto-Start Next Level. Hide WPM and/or the mistakes counter display while your typing. Play a sound when you hit the wrong key. Show the key you pressed if it was wrong (helps if you're avoiding looking at the keyboard.
