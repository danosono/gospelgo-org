Word

Type the entire Bible while improving your typing skills. Word is a touch typing game that helps you learn to type without looking at the keyboard.

Hours of fun and learning await you in Word. Give it a try!

Word uses the World English Bible (WEBP) translation.

Type

Learn type or improve your typing speed while you read the Bible.

Features

Track your progress. No penalty for reading the verse (5 second idle reset). See mistyped characters (optional). Press Delete to restart the level. Choose to display WPM and/or Mistakes, and much more!

Progress Tracking

Your progress is displayed on mulitple gameplay menus (Book, Chapter and Verse menu), and on the Progress Menu.

Cloud Save

Login with email and password to save your progress in the cloud. Play from any desktop browser on any PC/Mac without losing progress.
