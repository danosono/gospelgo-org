BubbleShooter

Bubble Shooter is a classic arcade game where the player aims and shoots colored bubbles to create matches of three or more of the same color. Free the bunny babies!

Bubbles

Match 3 or more bubbles of the same color to pop them. Clear the board to advance to the next level. Aim and shoot bubbles using your mouse (desktop browser) or touch (mobile browser).

Early Access

Levels

100 levels of increasing difficulty. Each level introduces new bubble arrangements and challenges. Can you clear them all?

Early Access

Powerups

Use powerups to clear bubbles more effectively. Explosives, color changers, and more! Strategically use powerups to beat tough levels and achieve high scores.

Early Access

Cloud Save Coming Soon

Login with email and password to save your progress in the cloud. Switch browsers (mobile or desktop) without losing progress. Also, some bug fixes are underway.
